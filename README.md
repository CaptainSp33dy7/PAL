# PAL (Peer Asset Lending)

![PAL Logo](./PAL_logo.png)

## Vision

**PAL** connects homeowners _(Lenders)_ who possess underutilized tools with local neighbors _(Borrowers)_ who need equipment for short-term projects. We solve the problem where Borrowers face high costs buying items for one-off jobs and deal with inconvenient rental shop logistics, while Lenders lose value on expensive assets sitting idle in garages. Through PAL, Borrowers benefit from affordable, instant access to professional tools right next door, while Lenders benefit by earning passive income and building a trusted community network.

---

## Stakeholders

1. **The Borrower** (The Neighbor)
2. **The Lender** (The Tool Owner)

## Problems Solved

- **The "Single-Use" Cost (Borrower):** Consumers often buy expensive equipment for one-off tasks, wasting money and storage space.
- **Inconvenient Logistics (Borrower):** Traditional rental shops are far away and have strict, inconvenient return hours.
- **Asset Idleness (Lender):** High-value tools sit unused in garages for most of their lifespan, depreciating without return.

## Key Benefits

- **Cost Savings:** Access professional tools for a fraction of the retail price.
- **Hyper-Convenience:** Pick up items from neighbors just a few doors down.
- **Passive Income:** Monetize idle assets that are gathering dust.
- **Community Trust:** Build social connections within the neighborhood.
